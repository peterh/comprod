About Commodity Producers

Story

You've decided to become a day trader. You've saved up a nest egg, and you are going to gamble it on the stock market. In particular, on the companies that produce commodities.

Game Play

Buy and sell board lots (blocks of 100) of the commodity producing companies listed on the game's market. We're pretending, so your broker works for free; there are no commissions payable on any trade.

Each day at midnight UTC, the game reflects the market adjustments for the day. Stock prices go up and down, and stocks doing well (at or above $100 per share) may even pay dividends.

At the end of each month, a winner is declared and the game is reset.
